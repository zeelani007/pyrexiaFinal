Debate

Rules:

Language: English

The competition will be conducted in two rounds-

  • Prelims
  • Finals

TOPIC: Prelims and finals topic will be given one night before event.

PRELIMS:

  • Each candidate will be speaking for/against (according to their wish) the motion for 3 minutes.
  • 4 best speakers for the motion and 4 best speakers against the motion will be shortlisted for the final round.
  • The results of the Prelims will be declared at the end of the event and the 8 shortlisted candidates will be asked to withdraw a chit which will decide the serial number (order of speaking) and whether he/she will speak in favour or against the given topic in the final.

FINALS:

  • Opening speech: 3 minutes each (warning bell at 2.5 minutes) The first speaker introduces the topic, stating arguments with reasoning and evidence, in the form of statistics or data to support his/her argument.
  • Cross question: 1 minute each Co-participants question the speaker. More than one question can be asked but it should be within time limit. Candidate who asked the question in rebuttal will not be judged.
  • Rebuttal: 2 mins each Questions put forth during cross examination are answered. The speaker may introduce new arguments.
  • Judges/Audience Questions: 2mins

No expression shall be accepted which is offensive, personal or defamatory in nature.

- Time limit in each section should be strictly adhered to. 1 point will be deducted from the total score for every 10 seconds beyond the time limit.

- Raise your point, not your voice, maintain the decorum of the debate.

- Facts presented in a debate must be accurate.

- The decision of judges would be final and cannot be challenged.

- Reading material can be taken on the stage.

Cognizzia: Test your knowledge

Rules:

  • The quiz is open to all . Teams can be of 2 or 3 members. Lone wolves are welcome too.
  • Each team should put their phones away. Any team considered to be cheating in any form would be disqualified instantly. Failure to report to the Quiz on time would also lead to disqualification from the quiz.
  • The Quiz will be held in 4 rounds. The 1st round will be a screening round on the basis of which 8 teams will be selected. In case of a tie , tie breaker questions will decide which team gets selected.
  • The 1st round will be a pen and paper round.
  • Unanswered questions in the last 3 rounds would be open to the audience. Upon answering correctly, you stand a chance of winning prizes.

Taboo

Rules:

  • A team will comprise of 4 members.
  • Each team will be given 5 minutes within which they will be asked to guess as many words as they can.
  • A Team chooses one of their players to be their first Clue-giver.
  • The clue giver will have to provide clues without using certain words which will be considered taboo. Usage of which will result in the disqualification of the team.
  • The team with maximum points wins.
