RULES AND REGUALTIONS :

ELIGIBILTY

  • -Students from any college are eligible for this competition.

  • -The team members chosen in the first round of the tournament will fix your team. Changing of team members in further rounds will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • -Team heads should write the names of their team members in the group before the start of their first round match.
  • -Each team should have 5 players.
  • -Teams not satisfying the above eligibility will be disqualified from the tournament.

TOURNAMENT TYPE

FIFA

Rules:

  • This competition is to be conducted under rules and regulations of PYREXIA.
  • There will be pools of registered players depending on the number of participants
  • Each pool will consist of knockout matches that will result in one winner
  • Winners of all the pools will be reaching the finals.
  • Knockout rounds will be conducted in the finals.
  • Draw matches will be decided by penalties
  • Absence of any one/both players at the time of the match will result in immediate disqualification
  • Participants are allowed to bring their own controller or keyboard
  • Matches will be held under supervision of the organising committee so any player violating standard rules will be disqualified.
