General Regulations

Basic registration is mandatory for all Players.

Insufficient registration can lead to cancellation of event.

(In case of cancellation registration fee will be refunded by coordinators).

Please take care of your kits and belongings, if lost, they will not be the management’s.

Teams are liable to disqualification against breach of disciplinary conduct if player/management are involved in illegal activities as per institution norms of AIIMS Rishikesh.

In all cases, the referee’s decision would be considered final. Not adhering to the referee’s decision will lead to disqualification of the team from the tournament.

Each player is strictly required to bring and deposit their college ID cards at the table in order to play the match.

NOTE: - In case of any discrepancy the final decision will be made by the referees after discussing with the officials.
