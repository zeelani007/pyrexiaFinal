  3. Basketball Rule

Only UG students and interns are allowed to play in the team

  • The tournament will be conducted according to FIBA rules.
  • Team will consist of max. 12 players, 5 will play at a given time.
  • If a team is unable to field 5 players till 15 minutes after the scheduled time of the game the opponent will get walk over.
  • Team should reach the court 15 minutes before the scheduled time of the match.
  • Each player is strictly required to bring and deposit their college ID cards at the table in order to play the match.
  • Teams are liable to disqualification against breach of disciplinary conduct if player/management are involved in illegal activities as per institution norms of AIIMS Rishikesh.
  • In all cases, the refree's decision would be considered final. Not adhering to the refree's decision will lead to disqualification of the team from the tournament.
Note* : In case of any discrepancy the final decision will be made by the referees after discussing with the officials.
