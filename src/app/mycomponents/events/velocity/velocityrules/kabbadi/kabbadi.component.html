Eligibility for Participation:

  • All players should be currently registered and studying in the same college.
  • All players MUST be upto or after 2018 batch ( any player before 2018 batch are NOT allowed to play)
  • All players must carry a valid college I-card before the start of every match.
  • In-case of protest against the admission of a player from a particular college, said player must produce copy of fee-receipt, current semester marksheet and website registration details as proof of admission within 2 hours of intimation to avoid disqualification.

General Regulations:

  • Teams are liable to disqualification against breach of disciplinary conduct.
  • Please take care of your kits and belongings, if lost, they will not be the management’s responsibility.
  • All teams must report at least 30 minutes before the scheduled time.
  • Kabbadi shoes will be provided during the play.
  • Shoes must be returned to the concerned, if not returned, fine would be imposed In all matches, the referee’s decision will be final.
  • In case of any dispute, the final decision rests with the organising committee.

Rules of Play:

  • Each team shall consist of max 12 players (min. 7 players).
  • Max.5 substitutions can be made during the game..
  • The duration of the matches is 40 minutes, played in two halves of 20 minutes (20-5-20).
  • In case of a tie , the match will go into extra time of 8 minutes.
  • The in-game rules will be explained before the matches to the teams by the referees and Co-ordinators.
  • *Insufficient registration can lead to cancellation of event.
  • (In case of cancellation registration fee will be refunded by coordinators)
