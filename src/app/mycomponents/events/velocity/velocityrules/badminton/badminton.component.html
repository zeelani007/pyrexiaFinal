Rules:

  • Rules will be according to BWF Rules (international rules). The tournament shall be played on knockout basis.
  • Each team has to bring their id card on court on match day .(mandatory)
  • Each team has to get their personal racquet.
  • We will provide the shuttlecocks. (Yonex Mavis 350)
  • Please take care of your kits and belongings. If lost they would not be the management’s responsibility.
  • All teams must report 30 minutes before the scheduled time.
  • Please respect the officials and other players. In all cases the, the referee’s decision would be final. Disputes will be resolved by the organising committee after discussions with the officials.
  • Teams are liable to disqualification against breach of disciplinary conduct if player/management are involved in illegal activities as per institute norms in AIIMS Rishikesh.
  • Toss winning side has to choose either to serve first or have desired side of court.

EVENTS:

1. MEN’S SINGLES-

  • Registration Fee- 200 per person
  • Knockout rounds will consist of 1 set of 21 points and from round of 16 onwards it will be best of three sets consisting of 21 points each set.

2. WOMEN’S SINGLES-

  • Registration Fee- 200 per person
  • Knockout rounds will consist of 1 set of 21 points and from round of 16 onwards it will be best of three sets consisting of 21 points each set.

3. MEN’S DOUBLES-

  • Registration Fee- 400 per team
  • Knockout rounds will consist of 1 set of 21 points and from round of 16 onwards it will be best of three sets consisting of 21 points each set.

4. WOMEN’S DOUBLES-

  • Registration Fee- 400 per team
  • Knockout rounds will consist of 1 set of 21 points and from round of 16 onwards it will be best of three sets consisting of 21 points each set.

5. MIXED DOUBLES-

  • Registration fees- 400 per team
  • Knockout rounds will consist of 1 set of 21 points and from round of 16 onwards it will be best of three sets consisting of 21 points each set.

6. Interval and Change of End-

  • When the leading team score reaches 11 points , players can have a 60 second interval.
  • A 2-minute interval between each game is allowed.
  • In the third game players change ends when the leading score reaches 11 points.
  • Each player can take an extra 1 min break in each game.

Important Points-

  • Referee or coordinators final decision will be taken case of any controversy.
  • Participants will be disqualified in case of failure to show up on time of the match
  • Depending upon the number of participations the number of sets and set point may change. This will be communicated to the player prior to the match.
  • Number of entries will be limited it will be on first come first serve basis.
  • INSUFFICIENT REGISTRATION MAY LEAD TO CANCELLATION OF EVENTS
