Rapid :

  • 15 minutes each side without increment

Blitz :

  • 5 minutes each side with 2 seconds increment per move

Bullet :

  • 2 minutes each with 1 second increment

Rules :

  • All rules will be according to standard FIDE laws of chess.
  • Just one illegal move will be allowed and second illegal move will result in loss of game. This rule is applicable for all three formats.
  • Chess clocks may or may not be provided,so kindly download chess clock app from Play Store.
  • Tournament will be conducted in knockout format.
  • Illegal moves should be reported to the arbiter .
  • The organisers will have the final authority over any discrepancies.
