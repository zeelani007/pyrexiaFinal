RULES FOR PLAY

  • Tournament will be conducted in knockout format.
  • All matches will be of 20 overs (maximum)
  • Each team can have a squad of 15 players (11) Tournament will be conducted in knockout format.*
  • Each team can have a squad of 15 players(11+4sub)
  • The playing 11 cannot be changed once submitted before the match.
  • The captain needs to give the list of playing 11 before the start of match.
  • No player can participate from 2 teams.If this occur, it would lead to the disqualification of both teams
  • AlI ICC rules will be followed except LBW .In case of rain D/L method as per icc rules is applicable.
  • During play, the umpires decision is the final decision
  • If any player didn't field for min 8 overs then he will not be eligible to bat or bowl for the remaining match
  • In case if any team does not reach at the ground for match at the scheduled time due to a valid reason, then in next 15 min they should be present otherwise the opponent will get bye. (If both the team don't reach then both will be disqualified)

Eligibility of participation:

  • All the player must be student of their representative College (ug 2018-2023,JR,SR).
  • All players should be registered as per fests basic registration rules.
  • All player must bring their identity card of their respective College.
  • In case of dispute against any player, player should produce valid documents (For UG- institute ID Card, marksheet, library card, Aadhaar Card ) For Residents, faculties- bank statement, joining letter. If he fails to produce team will be disqualified.
  • Only UGs of dental and nursing colleges can participate
  • SRs , JRs of MBBS colleges can participate

General Regulations:

  • Smoking or consumption of alcohol inside the ground are strictly prohibited, If found consuming such things, entire team will be disqualified from fest.
  • Misbehaviour with other team, sports committee, match umpires is prohibited if found team will be disqualified with immediate effect and no refund will be provided.
  • All matches will be played with white leather ball.
  • As much as possible, the team mates should wear same coloured jersey during the match.
  • White coloured jerseys are not allowed during match.
  • Metal spikes are not allowed, only normal sports shoes and rubber spikes are allowed.
  • Each team should get their personal kit including bat and practice ball.
  • We will provide only the match balls. No practice balls will be provided.
  • Please take care of your kits and belongings, if lost they would not be the managements responsibility.
  • Drinking water will be made available.
  • All teams must report 15mins before the scheduled time.
  • Please respect the officials and players in all cases.
  • The umpires decision would be final.
  • There is no specific age limit.
  • Scoring will be both online (cricheroes) and offline.
