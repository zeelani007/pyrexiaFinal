Eligibility for Participation:

  • All players should be currently registered and studying in the same college. (Only intern and ug students are allowed.)
  • All players must carry valid i-card before the start of every match.
  • In-case of protest against the admission of a player into a particular college, said player must current semester mark-sheet or website registration details as proof of admission within 4 hours of intimation to avoid disqualification.

General Regulations:

  • Teams are liable to disqualification against breach of disciplinary conduct if players/ management are involved in illegal activities as per institute norms in AIIMS Rishikesh.
  • Smoking/consumption of alcohol or any prohibited substances inside the campus are strictly prohibited. Entire team will be disqualified from the tournament even if a single player found to be violating these rules.
  • Each team has to bring their id card on court on match day.(mandatory)
  • Each team has to get their personal kits, proper kit is compulsory that is t-shirt, shorts, shoes.
  • We will provide the match balls.
  • Please take care of your kits and belongings, if lost they would not be the management’s responsibility.
  • Drinking water will be available.
  • All teams must report 30 Minutes before the scheduled time.
  • Please respect the officials and the other players. In all cases, the referee’s decision would be final. Disputes will be resolved by organising committee after discussions with the officials.
  • There is no specific age limit.

Rules of Play:

  • The rules of the International Volleyball Federation as adopted from time to time by the All India Volleyball Association shall apply, except rotation.
  • Each college must submit the list of players, not more than 12.
  • All matches will be knockouts and of 3 sets of 25 points, except semifinals and finals of 5 sets with last set of 15
  • The following points system will be followed for the matches played on league basis.
    • Match Won : 3 points
    • Match Lost : 0 point
    • Match abandoned : 1 point
  • Any team refusing to play match without justification will be eliminated from the competition and the results of the matches already played will be cancelled.
  • In case of teams having equal points the following equation will determine the ranking.
  • Z = Total number of sets won ÷ Total number of sets lost
  • The team with higher ratio “Z” will be ranked higher.
  • If the tie still remains, then the following system will be applied.
  • X = Total number of points won ÷ Total number of lost points.
  • The team with higher ratio “X” will be ranked higher.
  • If the tie still remains, precedence will be given to the team winning that match, in which they played against each other.
  • In no circumstances extra match will be played to determine which team ranked higher. The tally of all matches played in the league fixture will be considered.
