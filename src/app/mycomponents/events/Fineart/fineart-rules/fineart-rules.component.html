Fantasy Faces

Rules:

  • Participants must adhere to the designated theme for the competition. The theme will be announced before the competition begins.
  • This is a team event. Each team must comprise of two participants where one person will act as the canvas and the other as the artist. The roles cannot be switched once the competition starts.
  • Duration of the competition will be 1.5 hours
  • The participants will not be allowed to use any electronic devices during this period.
  • Acrylic colours and brushes will be provided.
  • Designs should be appropriate. Any design that is offensive or inappropriate will be disqualified.
  • Decision of the judges will be final and abiding.
