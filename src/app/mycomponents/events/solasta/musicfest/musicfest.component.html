Tarang- Indian Singing competition

Indian music boasts a rich tapestry of diverse styles and traditions, reflecting the country's cultural heritage. Classical music, with its two main genres - Hindustani and Carnatic, emphasizes intricate melodies and rhythmic patterns. Bollywood music is beloved for its fusion of traditional and contemporary elements in Indian film songs. Folk music showcases regional flavors and storytelling. Instruments like sitar, tabla, and veena add distinctive charm. Indian music transcends boundaries, touching hearts globally with its emotive expressions and captivating melodies, enthralling audiences with its time-honored artistry.

Metallica- Instrumental competition.

Vibrant and energetic street theatre music, free from vocals, relies solely on instruments to convey emotions and tell stories. Widely used in soundtracks, instrumental music sets the tone for movies, games, and events. 8 MILE (Rap battle and beat boxing)

Beatboxing, a vocal art form, mimics drum sounds and beats using the mouth, voice, and lips, creating rhythmic patterns and music. It complements rap battles, where MCs engage in lyrical contests, showcasing their rhyming skills and wit. Together, beatboxing and rap battles form an electrifying fusion of vocal prowess and wordplay, captivating audiences with their impressive displays of talent and creativity. These dynamic expressions of hip-hop culture have become a global phenomenon, empowering artists to push the boundaries of vocal performance and entertain with their unique rhythmic abilities.
