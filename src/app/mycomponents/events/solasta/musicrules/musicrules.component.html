Metallica - Instrumental competition.

Rules :

  • Each performance should have one, or a maximum of two participants. If two members are there, one will be judged for the act and the other will be there for assistance only.
  • Maximum of two instruments are allowed per act
  • Only drum kit and keyboard will be provided. Participants are requested to bring their own drum sticks or any other instrument other than the above two mentioned.
  • Judges decision will be final Any wrong means for the enhancement of the performance or any act considered morally wrong would lead to immediate disqualification.

Battle of Bands

Rules :

Eligibility:

  • The competition is open to all bands formed by current college or university undergraduate students.
  • Each band must have a minimum of 3 members and a maximum of 7 members who are currently enrolled students.

Registration:

  • Bands must register through the official college event website or designated registration platform.
