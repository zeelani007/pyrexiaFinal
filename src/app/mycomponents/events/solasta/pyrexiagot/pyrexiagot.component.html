Eligibility:

Only registered participants of the college fest are eligible to participate in the talent round. Ensure that participants have valid identification to verify their enrolment in the college.

The entry should contain:

  • Name of the participant
  • Batch of the participant
  • Contact no of the participant
  • College of the participant
  • Screenshot of the payment

There will be single round in the competition

The time limit is expected to be 3 mins but it can negotiate as per act. Please contact the respective organisers for the purpose.

Only those acts which do not fall under any of the competition being organised have to register for the competition

Participants are required to carry their own props and instruments if needed.

Any performance that includes vulgar language, offensive gestures, or explicit content is prohibited.

Safety Measures: If any act involves activities that may pose risks, such as fire performances or acrobatics, require participants to provide a detailed safety plan for their act

Judging Criteria includes factors like creativity, stage presence, technical skill, audience engagement, and overall performance.

judges' decisions are final and cannot be challenged or appealed.
